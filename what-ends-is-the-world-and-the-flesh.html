What ends is the world and the flesh

September 10, 2023 — Levi Searches

August 8, 2023

Most people live most of their lives - or all of it - enthralled in a constant litany, in a constant noise. Earthly noise. Noise of mundane, everyday, and immediate worries. As if life were to end in the next moment.

Most do not realize the au-del√† of life. Life does not end today, nor tomorrow, nor in the next decade. Life never ends; what ends is the world.

What comes after a bill? Another bill? And another, and another, and so on, until the grave puts an end to it? Does life end with the next bite or the next coin, the next pleasure or the next pain? Life never ends; what ends is the world and the flesh.
